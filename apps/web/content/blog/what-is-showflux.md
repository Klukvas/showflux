## The Challenge of Modern Real Estate Management

Running a real estate business involves juggling dozens of moving parts: active listings, scheduled showings, incoming offers, team coordination, and client communication. Most brokerages rely on a patchwork of spreadsheets, email threads, and disconnected tools that slow everything down.

ShowFlux was built to solve this problem.

## What ShowFlux Does

ShowFlux is an all-in-one real estate management platform designed specifically for brokers and agents. It brings your entire workflow into a single, intuitive dashboard.

### Listing Management

Create and manage property listings with all the details that matter — address, price, status, bedrooms, bathrooms, square footage, and more. Track each listing through its lifecycle from draft to active to under contract to sold.

### Showing Scheduling

Schedule and coordinate property showings without the back-and-forth. Each showing is linked to a listing, assigned to an agent, and tracked with status updates. No more missed appointments or double-bookings.

### Offer Tracking

When offers come in, capture every detail: price, contingencies, financing type, expiration dates. Compare multiple offers side by side and move the best ones forward with confidence.

### Team Collaboration

Invite agents to your workspace, assign roles, and keep everyone on the same page. Brokers get a bird's-eye view of team activity while agents focus on their assigned listings and showings.

## Who Is ShowFlux For?

ShowFlux is designed for two key roles in real estate:

- **Brokers** who manage teams, oversee multiple listings, and need visibility across their entire operation
- **Agents** who handle day-to-day showings, communicate with buyers, and track offers on their assigned properties

Whether you run a boutique brokerage with three agents or a growing firm with dozens, ShowFlux scales with your business.
